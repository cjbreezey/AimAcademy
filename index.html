AIM ACADEMY

Aim Academy is designed to help improve your mouse speed and accuracy. The rules are simple, use your mouse to click Objects that appear on screen. Your goal is to accurately click it as quick as possible. There are 3 game modes; Timed, Survival, and God mode. When ready, press Start to choose your game mode.

Timed Mode - Practice how many objects you can click in the given Time
Survival Mode - Score as many points you can with 3 lives
God Mode - Can you survive the wrath of God?
